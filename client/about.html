    What's up! My name is Jeremy Mitchell, this whole website is based around golfing and jokes. There are a few things you should know about me, so here they are.

  As a child, I always enjoyed quiet activities. I have never been one for large, and loud gatherings. I have always much preffered smaller, more intimate groups, where the quality of time spent is much greater.

  Now that I am older, I have adopted several hobbies, with golf being the main one. Which is why I have made this blog-esque website. In my opinion the best part of golfing is the time you get to spend with those you care about. There are few things in life that will separate you from the rest of the world for a few hours like a game of golf. I know that some may say playing music on the course is poor etiquette, but I think it adds to the level of relaxation, so long as it's played in moderation.
