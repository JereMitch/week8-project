Callaway Maverick Max Iron Set:  This is my personal set of irons that I use! I love these bad boys. They have a good solid weight to the head of the club, and a good amount of spring in the shaft. I would definitely recommend this iron-set to all of my friends. The other positive thing is if you're anything ilke me, your swing goes from right to left.

Ping G2 Driver:  Now let me tell you WHAT. This here driver, so long as you have the 15.5 degree loft, will send your golf ball into orbit. I know it's not ideal, but if you want your drives to go sky high, this is your driver. I was out golfing with my dad and brothers the other day, and I'm pretty sure I knocked a satelite out of orbit. I still haven't even mentioned the best part! The sound, ooooooh, the sound this sucker makes is crisp, clear, and loud as a mf'er. Even if you duff your drive, the whole course will hear this crack.

Thrift Shop Clubs:  Good ol' thrift shops and their old janky golf clubs. This is the way to golf with some good buddies on a small town course with no one around. I love these for the surprise factor, you never know what you're gonna get! Will there be a good driver? Will you actually find the club you're hoping for? The world may never know. However, when you get lucky at a thrift shop, you freaking get lucky. I love thrift shop clubs, I once got a $350 driver for $35, simply because no one knew.
